Problem Statement

Urban residents want to recycle more effectively, but inconsistent local recycling rules and lack of clear information make it confusing to know what items can be recycled.

Our affinity diagram organizes common recycling challenges into categories: Lack of Information, Inconsistent Policies, Behavioral Challenges, and Environmental Impact.
